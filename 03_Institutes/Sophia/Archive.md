## Archive Structure

### Ethics Assessment Register
All ethics assessments conducted, with: subject, date, risk rating, decision.
The reasoning chain for each decision is preserved in full.

### Philosophical Frameworks Library
Documented frameworks for recurring types of philosophical analysis.
Prevents rebuilding frameworks from scratch for known question types.

### Strategic Frameworks Library
Machiavel's strategic frameworks for recurring decision types.
Reference for future strategic analyses.

### Historical Precedent Index
Herodotus's historical research, indexed by topic and relevance.

### Ethics Framework Evolution Log
A record of how `00_Constitution/Ethics.md` has changed over time and why.
Ensures the evolution of AION's ethical framework is always traceable.
