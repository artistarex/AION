## Overview

The DaVinci Archive preserves DaVinci Studio's creative outputs, decisions, and standards.

Maintained by: Gallery (Archivist)
Access: All AION agents (read), DaVinci agents (full)

---

## Archive Structure

### AION Visual Identity Standards
The official visual standards for all AION-produced materials.
Version-controlled. Prism maintains; Gallery archives.

**Current standards (v0.1.0):**
- Background: Dark (`#1a1a2e` / `#0f0f1a`)
- Accent: Gold (`#c9a84c`)
- Display font: Cormorant Garamond
- Body font: Inter
- Mono font: Space Mono
- Tone: Premium, precise, dark luxury

### Completed Projects Register
All DaVinci projects: task ID, type, date, client/operator, outcome.

### Style Guides Library
Style guides produced for specific operators or contexts.
Enables consistent creative work across sessions.

### Creative Brief Library
Approved creative briefs and their corresponding outcomes.
Used for reference when similar briefs arrive.

### Design Decision Log
Significant design decisions and their rationale.
Enables future agents to understand why standards are what they are.
