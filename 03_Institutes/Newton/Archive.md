## Overview

The Newton Archive is the scientific knowledge repository of the Newton Institute.
Maintained by: Mendel (Archivist)
Access: All AION agents (read), Newton agents (full)

---

## Archive Structure

### Completed Research Register
Index of all Newton research outputs: task ID, topic, date, verification level, QC score.

### Source Library
Curated list of high-quality sources used in Newton research.
Each source has: name, type, classification, reliability notes.

### Methodology Library
Documented research methodologies proven effective for specific question types.
Used to accelerate future research on similar topics.

### Contested Findings
Findings where sources conflict, labeled `Contested`.
Preserved for future resolution as new evidence emerges.

### Verification Certificates
Archive of all Socrates verification certificates issued.
Reference for cross-institute trust decisions.

---

## Knowledge Contribution Process

When a Newton finding is ready for the Global Knowledge Base:
1. Mendel formats using `Knowledge_Entry_Template.md`
2. Socrates confirms verification status
3. Archimedes approves
4. Submitted to Sophia for Global Knowledge Base entry
5. Logged in Completed Research Register
