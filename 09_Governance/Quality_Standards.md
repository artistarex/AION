## Overview

These standards are operationalized in `01_Sophia/QualityControl.md`.

---

## Universal Output Standards

Every output from every institute must meet:

### Standard 1 — Accuracy
**Requirement:** All factual claims are correct to the best of AION's verified knowledge.
**Measurement:** % of claims that are verified (target: 100% for Approved outputs)
**Minimum:** No unverified claim may be presented without a confidence label

### Standard 2 — Completeness
**Requirement:** The output fully addresses the task as defined in the brief.
**Measurement:** All stated sub-questions answered, no major gaps
**Minimum:** All required sections present per the relevant template

### Standard 3 — Clarity
**Requirement:** The output is understandable by its intended audience without additional explanation.
**Measurement:** Reviewer can understand the output without asking clarifying questions
**Minimum:** No undefined jargon, logical structure, clear conclusion

### Standard 4 — Sourcing
**Requirement:** All factual claims are traceable to a cited source.
**Measurement:** Source list complete, every claim maps to a source number
**Minimum:** All claims labeled with appropriate confidence level

### Standard 5 — Format
**Requirement:** Output uses the correct template, includes all metadata, follows naming conventions.
**Measurement:** Checklist compliance
**Minimum:** Template used, metadata complete, version numbered

|----------|-------------|

## Quality Score Reference

| Score | Label | Meaning | Action |
|-------|-------|---------|--------|
| 5.0 | Excellent | Exceeds all standards | Approve + note as exemplary |
| 4.0–4.9 | Good | Meets all standards | Approve |
| 3.0–3.9 | Acceptable | Meets minimum standards | Return for specific improvements |
| 2.0–2.9 | Below Standard | Significant gaps | Reject — significant revision |
| 1.0–1.9 | Inadequate | Does not meet minimum | Reject — redo from brief |

---

## Quality Trend Management

Sophia tracks quality scores over time.

**Warning threshold:** Institute average below 3.5 over 3 consecutive outputs
**Action:** Director briefed, improvement plan required within one session

**Critical threshold:** Institute average below 3.0 over 5 consecutive outputs
**Action:** Institute placed in Review status, human operator notified
