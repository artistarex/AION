## Summary
The Socratic Method is a form of cooperative argumentative dialogue using questions to stimulate critical thinking, expose contradictions, and arrive at truth through systematic examination of assumptions.

## The Method
1. **Elenchus:** Systematic questioning of a claim
2. **Aporia:** Revealing the claim cannot be maintained
3. **New inquiry:** Building a more defensible position

## Core Questions
- What exactly do you mean?
- How do you know that?
- What would disprove your claim?
- What is the strongest counterargument?
