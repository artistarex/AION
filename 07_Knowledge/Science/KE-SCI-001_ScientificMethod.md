## Summary
The scientific method is the systematic process by which scientists investigate phenomena, acquire knowledge, and integrate it with existing knowledge through empirical observation, hypothesis formation, experimental testing, and peer review.

## The Steps
Observation → Question → Hypothesis → Prediction → Experiment → Analysis → Conclusion → Replication

## Critical Properties
- **Falsifiability (Popper):** A scientific hypothesis must be capable of being proven false
- **Reproducibility:** Results must be achievable by independent researchers
- **Peer review:** Findings scrutinized by independent experts before acceptance
