## Summary
History offers consistent patterns on organizational success and failure. Successful organizations share clear purpose, defined roles, accountability, and adaptability. Failed ones share opacity, role confusion, unaccountable power, and rigidity.

## Patterns of Success
1. **Clear mandate boundaries** — know exactly what you do and don't do
2. **Distributed expertise + coordinated authority** — specialist excellence with effective coordination
3. **Documented institutional memory** — decisions outlast individuals
4. **Accountability without micromanagement** — hold accountable for outcomes, not methods

## Patterns of Failure
1. **Concentrated authority** — single points of failure
2. **Opacity of decision-making** — cannot learn from undocumented mistakes
3. **Rigidity** — failure to adapt when environment changes

## Application to AION
- Distributed authority → institute system vs. single AI
- Documented reasoning → all decisions logged
- Defined boundaries → institute mandates
- Sophia as coordinator, not dictator
